BENTOTA TURTLE HATCHERY


Turtle Breeding In Bentota


Turtle breeding in Bentota is a significant conservation effort aimed at protecting and preserving endangered sea turtles. The Bentota Turtle Hatchery plays a vital role in this process by collecting turtle eggs from nearby beaches and providing a safe environment for them to hatch. The eggs are carefully incubated under controlled conditions, ensuring optimal temperature and humidity levels for successful hatching. Once the baby turtles emerge, they are nurtured in protected tanks, receiving proper care and nutrition until they are strong enough to be released into the ocean.




Bentota Beach


Bentota Beach is a popular tourist destination located in the southern coastal region of Sri Lanka. It is known for its pristine sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and stunning natural beauty. Here's some information about Bentota Beach. Location: Bentota Beach is situated in the town of Bentota, which is approximately 65 kilometers south of Colombo, the capital city of Sri Lanka. It is part of the Galle District. Beach Activities: The beach offers a wide range of activities for visitors to enjoy. These include swimming, sunbathing, snorkeling, diving, jet skiing, banana boat rides, and boat cruises along the Bentota River.



THINGS YOU CAN DO IN BENTOTA

In Bentota, Sri Lanka, there is a wide range of activities to indulge in and attractions to explore. Relax on the pristine beaches, basking in the sun or taking refreshing dips in the clear turquoise waters. Engage in thrilling water sports like snorkeling, diving, jet skiing, and windsurfing. Embark on a mesmerizing river safari along the Bentota River, witnessing the beauty of the mangroves and spotting wildlife. Visit the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery to learn about turtle conservation efforts and release baby turtles into the ocean. Immerse yourself in Ayurvedic wellness with rejuvenating spa treatments. Explore cultural sites such as the Brief Garden, Galapatha Raja Maha Vihara temple, and Lunuganga Estate, all offering unique glimpses into Sri Lanka's rich heritage. With its diverse offerings, Bentota ensures a memorable experience for all travelers.
